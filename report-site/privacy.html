Privacy Policy

We keep this simple, because what we collect is simple.

What we collect. When you sign up to receive the report or request a briefing, we collect what you type into the form: your name, email address, and — if you choose to provide them — your organization and role.

Why. We use your email to send you the report when it's published and occasional updates from the author. Briefing requests go to the author's team so they can respond. Nothing else. We don't sell or share your information.

Who processes it. Form submissions are processed and stored by Netlify, which hosts this site.

Your choices. Every email we send includes a way to unsubscribe. To have your information deleted, email info@blueby2032.com and we'll take care of it.
